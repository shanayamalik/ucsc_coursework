Spot the Llama!

There's a herd of llamas enjoying the outdoor nature together. However, one special llama is unlike the others, wearing a black and white checkered pattern on the skin, rather than the usual off-white fur that often coats a llama. However, the shrubs are quickly growing, making it harder and harder to spot the llama. Your mission is to navigate through this world using the keys listed above to find the unique llama. Once you spot the unique llama with your eyes, press the spacebar to claim your victory!
